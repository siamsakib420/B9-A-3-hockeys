Professional Program

Professional ice hockey (hockey) is the competition of ice hockey in which participants are paid to play.
Professional competition began in North America in the United States—in Pennsylvania and Michigan—and in Canada around 1900.
Professional ice hockey expanded across Canada and the United States and eventually to many other countries. There are major leagues around the world, including the National Hockey League in North America
